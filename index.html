Akira Toriyama

Creator of the popular series Dragon Ball

Toriyama and some of his character designs
  • 1978 - At age 23, Toriyama quit his job and entered the manga industry by publishing his first series, Wonderland & Wonderland 2
  • 1980 - Created Dr. Slump, which became serialized in Weekly Shōnen Jump from 1980-1984
  • 1983 - The series Dragon Boy was born and later evolved into the series Dragon Ball
